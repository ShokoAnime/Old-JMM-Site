Privacy Policy

We want to make the following information as clear as possible to address any potential concerns any user might have on what data Anime Buddy does and does not collect and what sites Anime Buddy connects to for information. If you feel we've missed something or have a question then please let us know.

Your Personal Information

Anime Buddy does not store, share or publish any of your personal information nor is it used for any type of personal or financial gain. Your personal information belongs to you and we want to keep it that way.

Connecting To The Internet

Anime Buddy will automatically download data from select websites for use with the calendar and your collection. Each site as well as what data is downloaded is listed below.
  • Azure - calendar and search information - http://jmm.azurewebsites.net/
  • AniDB - detailed information about an anime - http://anidb.net
  • TheTvDB - fanart backgrounds (images only) - http://thetvdb.com/

Connecting To Local Network

If you've chosen to connect Anime Buddy with JMM Server, Anime Buddy will use this connection to display your collection information from your local database.
