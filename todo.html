Website To-Do List

Below you'll find a a list of pages that need updating and/or pages that are missing. Items on the to-do list have been placed in their respective categories for easier navigation, a crossed out item means it's already been completed. To avoid duplicates, edit the item on this page you are working on. For additional help and templates check the JMM Website Guide page.

We truly appreciate the help!

In order to facilitate work on the website, we've placed tasks into different stages.

Stage 1: Redesign website making it more user friendly and responsive.
Stage 2: Finish remaining pages, design is not important and instead focus on content.
Stage 3: Rework designs making long pages or pages with a lot of information easier to read and navigate.
