Anime Buddy Privacy Policy

Your Personal Information

Anime Buddy does not store, share or publish any of your personal information.

Connecting to the internet

Anime Buddy connects to the following sources to get Information
  • Azure - calendar and search information - http://jmm.azurewebsites.net/
  • AniDB - detailed information about an anime - http://anidb.net
  • TheTvDB - fanart backgrounds (images only) - http://thetvdb.com/

Connecting to local network

If you chosen to connect to JMM Server, Anime Buddy will use this connection to display information from your local database.
