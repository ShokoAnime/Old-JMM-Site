Support

We've setup a wide range of resources for those looking for support with one of the JMM programs or plugins.

Live Chat On Discord

Come chat with us on our Discord server! Click the link in the menu above (mobile users click the mobile menu button) or Click Here to open Discord. Even if no one is active in the chat still post your question, messages do not disappear and someone will reply when they get on.

Online Documentation

Our online documentation provides detailed information on the JMM suite, if you're not receiving an error message than check this first. Please note we make every effort to update the documentation with the latest versions of JMM. However the online documentation may contain outdated or missing data. If what you're looking for is not there, lets us know and we'll add it to our list or you can add it yourself.

JMM Server

My Anime 3
