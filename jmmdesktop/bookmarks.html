Bookmarks Tab

Bookmarks are a great way to keep track of shows you're interested in to download at a later point. In addition you can queue up bookmarked series to download using JMM and uTorrent.


Adding Bookmarks

Currently JMM Desktop only supports adding bookmarks by viewing a series in your collection's Related and Similar section in the Collections tab.

To add a bookmark, click on the Bookmark icon that's to the right of the pinned icon. There is no confirmation message when you add a bookmark, you'll only get a message if you try to bookmark a series you already have bookmarked. Once you've bookmarked a series click the Bookmarks tab at the top, this will allow you to manage your bookmarks

Managing Bookmarks

Currently there's two different types of bookmarks lists, Downloading and Not Downloading. You do not need to sort your bookmarks into one of the two categories and instead can leave them in their default category of Not Downloading. You can make changes to each bookmarked series using the options explained below.

Red X - Located to the right of the series name, click this button will delete the bookmark.
Download Now - If clicked will bring you to the Search Torrent section under Downloads allowing you to download the bookmarked series. Keep in mind this will search based off the series name so a series with multiple movies or OVA's will have everything show up.
Not Downloading - This is the state of the bookmarked series and is more for your benefit of keeping track of what bookmarks you've downloaded or haven't downloaded.
Priority - Changing this number will change your bookmarked series order. Once you've changed the priorities click the Refresh button to see your changes. By default the priority is set to 1 and your list is sorted by added order.
Edit Notes - Allows you to input notes for the bookmarked series. For example what fansub group to download or release date for the next OVA or Movie.
